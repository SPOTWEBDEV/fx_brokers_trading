    TradingView Charting and Trading Platform

    TradingView’s financial analysis platform delivers seamless access to the global markets. From the world's most traded currency pairs to small-cap stocks, and more than 50 million users globally that make up a large and well-organised social trading community, TradingView remains the go-to charting solution for all experience levels.

    What is TradingView and
    How Does it Work?

    What is TradingView and How Does it Work?

    TradingView provides advanced charting functionality through desktop, web-based browsers and mobile devices. With many traders adopting different trading styles and navigating various markets using different strategies, TradingView users have the freedom to explore and employ more than 100,000 public indicators.

    Boasting a straightforward login process, easy-to-use charting, and the choice to create custom layouts as well as write and share your indicators and strategies with TradingView’s Pine Script, TradingView is a platform that operates with the trader and investor in mind.

    Track and Trade
    Global Markets with TradingView

    Track and Trade Global Markets with TradingView

    With TradingView, traders can track and trade a wide selection of markets. The platform provides comprehensive real-time coverage for global markets, including Stocks, Bonds, Forex, Digital Currencies, Commodities and Indices.

    The platform also allows users to easily switch between different financial instruments, employ multi-chart modes, apply various technical analysis tools and indicators specific to each market, conduct correlation analysis through TradingView’s overlay function and access comprehensive fundamental data and more. Regardless of whether you're a multi-market trader or interested in exploring new markets, TradingView supplies the tools and resources needed to track and trade financial markets effectively.

    Why Choose TradingView?

    User-Friendly Interface:
    Delivering a clear and responsive platform, TradingView boasts an intuitive interface that is straightforward to navigate and customise. The platform offers flexibility in layout, colour schemes and technical indicators, enabling users to tailor their trading environment to their preferences.
    Advanced Charting Tools:
    TradingView offers a variety of charting tools and technical indicators that are user-friendly and innovative, serving both novice and experienced traders. These tools range from basic support and resistance applications to advanced Fibonacci ratios.
    Social Community:
    Known worldwide for its active social community of traders and investors, TradingView allows users to share educational posts, analysis, and video ideas and even follow other traders to gain insights and inspiration. TradingView's vast library of community-generated content offers a wealth of educational material.
    Real-Time Data and News:
    TradingView provides real-time market data, including price quotes, volume, and other relevant information. Users can also access global news feeds and economic calendars, keeping them updated on market events that may impact their trading.

    FAQs

    What is TradingView?

    TradingView is an online platform for traders and investors. It offers real-time data, customisable charts, technical analysis tools and a large social community to share ideas and insights.

    How Do I Create a TradingView Account?

    To create a TradingView account, visit their website. Then, click the ‘Sign Up’ tab and select an email or social media option. From here, input the necessary details and agree to the terms to gain access to your account and start using TradingView.

    How Do I Fund My TradingView Account?

    You can fund your TradingView account through the FP Markets client portal.

    How Do I View Charts on TradingView?

    1. Log in to your TradingView account.

    2. Use the Search Bar (Ctrl+K) to find the desired financial instrument.

    3. Select the instrument (Symbol) to Launch its chart.

    4. Customise the chart to your preferences.

    Why is TradingView Popular?

    Having the flexibility to explore different markets and engage with like-minded traders is why TradingView is so popular. TradingView’s large social community enables idea-sharing and learning across a wide range of markets, with access to a well-designed chat room for each market.
