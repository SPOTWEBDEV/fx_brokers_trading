    Trading Central

    Internationally recognised by the Investorside Research Association and the European Association of Independent Research Providers, Trading Central is an unsurpassed third-party source of information and analysis.

    Although unavailable to retail traders, Trading Central can be accessed through an authorised brokerage company, such as FPMarkets.

    What is Trading Central and How Does it Work?

    What is Trading Central and How Does it Work?

    Trading Central delivers a suite of analytical tools, including an in-depth Economic Calendar, Market Buzz (News Analytics and Sentiment Research), Technical Views and Daily Newsletters to stay up to date with current economic and political affairs across the financial space. Through these specialised facilities, market participants can anticipate and act on market-moving events, view key value investment metrics, harness online news with powerful AI technology, and receive investment ideas that are trusted across the industry.

    Trading Central works with a user-friendly platform, allowing users to freely navigate its various offerings. For example, Technical Views, led by senior analysts, constantly monitors the financial markets for actionable insights and displays the results through an easy-to-read menu at regular intervals.

    Why Use Trading Central?

    Why Use Trading Central?

    Trading Central, at its core, provides an unmatched research solution, delivering essential tools to generate informed trading decisions and consequently aid trading strategies. For beginners especially, the software produces invaluable data and offers an in-depth understanding of the market’s direction with real-time economic data.

    Supported by senior analyst experts, advanced automated algorithms, and proprietary trading pattern recognition systems, Trading Central constantly monitors the market, leading to actionable trading plans based on award-winning methodologies.

    Automatic Technical and Fundamental Analysis

    Automatic Technical and Fundamental Analysis

    We live in an Information Age. New providers churn out news at a rapid rate, which can be overwhelming. Automatic technical and fundamental analysis tools remove the process of navigating through data to arrive at an actionable source. Trading Central achieves this through AI-driven ideas that provide simple interpretations of complex financial data.

    Trading Central FAQ

    What is Trading Central?

    Trading Central is a leading source of information for financial traders and investors. The company represents an award-winning sophisticated blend of technical and fundamental analytical tools that work 24 hours daily to enable a deeper breadth of market coverage.

    How do I create a Trading Central account?

    Creating a Trading Central account is straightforward. Open a Live Trading Account with FPMarkets and access Trading Central’s software through your dedicated Client Portal.

    Is Trading Central accurate?

    Yes, Trading Central is an accurate source of information.

    The long answer. Yes, Trading Central is recognised by the highest data analyst authorities in the world. It continues to assist market-leading companies to win global awards in wealth management, personal finance and economic research, which has made Trading Central one of the most reliable sources of financial intelligence.

    How do you use a Trading Central indicator?

    Trading Central provides investors with signals that aid analysis across multiple asset classes, encompassing an array of analytical approaches. Trading Central filters copious amounts of data to separate wanted from unwanted information, allowing investors to draw their own conclusions based on individual preferences.

    The software combines years of research experience and time-tested indicators, supported by highly qualified professionals that use unique and sophisticated methodologies. This provides all types of investors with the necessary tools to make informed trading decisions, regardless of their approach to trading. So, how someone uses Trading Central depends on their personal goals and trading approach.

    Does Technical Analysis work in trading?

    Like any tool, technical analysis is as effective as the person using it. A seasoned trader with a developed risk-management strategy and a mature psychological makeup, can use data produced by technical analysis more effectively than a less experienced trader. But it is not just for professional users. The study of technical analysis can serve as an effective way for beginner traders to start familiarising themselves with economic principles and identifying signals and trends.
